About Me

I’m Kazi Rifat Morshed (International Phonetic Alphabet: /ˈkɑːziː ˈriːfæt ˈmɔːrʃɛd/; Pronunciation: "ka-zee ree-fath mor-shed", Bengali: কাজী রিফাত মোর্শেদ), an undergraduate student of Computer Science and Engineering, currently studying in Khulna University, Khulna, Bangladesh 🇧🇩. I grew a firm enthusiasm for technology (especially Computers) when my father brought me an Intel Pentium Duel Core computer in 2009. Without access to the internet, I explored different aspects of computers and video games. After completing high school, I got access to the internet opening the door towards further exploration.
Though I first used Linux in my desktop PC in 2015 which was Ubuntu 15.04 Vivid Vervet, I completely moved to Linux in November 2021. In August 2023, I got admitted into my dream subject, Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University. The Computer Science and Engineering Discipline of Khulna University is the second oldest CSE Department of Bangladesh,ref established in 1991 after the CSE Department of Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 1982.
