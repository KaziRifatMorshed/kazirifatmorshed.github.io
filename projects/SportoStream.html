SportoStream

MILESTONE: Team CSE KU has streamed a match with its own developed scoring system software

SportoStream is a sports streaming and scoring system software developed to live-stream matches with a dedicated, in-house scoring overlay.

The Backstory

Last term, while chatting in class, my classmate Sajal Mondal suggested we build our own software to stream our team's cricket matches. Since I had experience with the Qt framework, I told him I'd look into it.

In November 2025, right in the middle of our term finals, the idea suddenly clicked. After some research and prototyping, I realized: We can actually do this. I immediately texted Sajal, and we agreed to build it together in December.

Despite a tight deadline and a setback when Sajal was injured by a cricket ball, we pushed through. Even while recovering, Sajal made an incredible contribution — without him, completing this would have been much tougher.

We are proud to announce that we now have our own dedicated software for our sports streaming!

Team Members

Key Features

  • Custom-built scoring system for cricket, football, volleyball, table tennis matches
  • Live streaming overlay integration
  • Real time scoring update in website hosted in a remote server.
